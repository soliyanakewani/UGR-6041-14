This is me. Anyone who knows me knows how I am obsessed with taking pictures. Capturing the moment on my phone makes me happier. Even if it is my profession, I still don't see it as work but my freedom. Looking back at the photos I took makes me realize it is just a matter of being seen and an angle to be seen that will make things more beautiful.

In addition to photography, poetry is also my all-time favorite hobby. I take what the world gave me through pictures and I show the world my insides through my poems. Ever since I was little, I have difficulty expressing my emotions in the moment of action, but afterward, it was a beautiful moment of me communicating through my pens with the papers and not real people. And that was how I developed my skill of poetry. I have more than 100 poems lying one on top of the other in my drawer waiting to be told to the world who made a contribution for their creation.

The lyric of this holy mezmur is the best one I know so far that I could guarantee 100% the best poem ever. Even though it is not a poem itself, the lyric is more powerful for me.
